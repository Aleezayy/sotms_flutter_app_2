# Privacy Policy for SOTMS

**Last Updated: July 01, 2026**

SOTMS ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Smart Oil Transport Monitoring System mobile application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### A. Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information includes:
* Email address
* First name and last name
* Company name and role details

#### B. Location Data
We require access to location data to monitor the transit of oil transport vehicles. 
* **ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION**: The app collects precise and approximate location data to track the GPS coordinates of the tankers.
* **Background Location**: Location data may be collected in the background to ensure continuous route tracking even when the app is minimized, as required for fleet monitoring.

---

## 2. Use of Data
SOTMS uses the collected data for various purposes:
* To provide and maintain our fleet monitoring service
* To notify users about updates and critical safety alerts
* To display live tanker locations on maps
* To monitor driver behavior (e.g., speed, sudden braking)
* To provide customer support and manage user accounts

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* All account details and location telemetry are stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
* Our backend utilizes secure cloud databases (Google Firebase).
* We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your personal information.

---

## 4. Disclosure of Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may disclose your personal data in the following situations:
* **Compliance with Laws**: To comply with a legal obligation.
* **Service Providers**: We may employ third-party service providers (such as Google Firebase for database storage and map rendering) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

---

## 5. Your Data Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
* Access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
* Opt-out of location tracking (note: this may disable key tracking functions of the application).
