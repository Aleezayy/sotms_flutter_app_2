## 3. Full Description
**SOTMS (Smart Oil Transport Monitoring System) is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade fleet management solution designed specifically for oil transport operations.** (Max 4000 characters)

Keep track of your oil tankers, optimize delivery routes, and ensure safety and compliance with live GPS tracking and smart driver behavior alerts.

### Key Features:
* **Real-time GPS Tracking**: Monitor your fleet on an interactive map with custom routes, live vehicle markers, and precise ETA updates.
* **Enterprise Fleet Dashboard**: Get a high-level overview of active transport runs, average speeds, total cargo quantity, and on-time delivery metrics.
* **Instant Safety Alerts**: Get immediate critical notifications for over-speeding, sudden braking, unexpected route deviations, and fuel anomalies.
* **Detailed Vehicle Profiles**: View detailed specs for each tanker, current cargo details, assignment status, and driver assignments.
* **User-friendly Settings**: Toggle between Light and Dark modes, configure notifications, and select language preferences (full English and Urdu localization).

Optimize your logistics, secure your cargo, and protect your drivers with SOTMS.
